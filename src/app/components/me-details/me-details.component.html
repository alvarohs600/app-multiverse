About tha author:

My name is Álvaro Hontoria Sánchez, and I am the creator of the Multiverse app. I consider myself a creative and hardworking person, before studying programming, I studied fine arts at the Complutense University of Madrid. At first I thought about developing a mobile application for the end of the course project, but everything changed after the start of the training practices in workplaces, where I started working on web development with the Angular framework. At that moment I thought it would be a good learning system to try to develop a web application, with the challenge that this meant, since my studies at the IES SAMPEDRO center were related to multiplatform application development. Another challenge of creating a web application, was to learn a new programming language, Typscript, which works with the Angular framework. Despite all the difficulties, this experience has been extremely enriching on a personal and intellectual level, as it has allowed me to expand my knowledge and skills in web development, gain confidence in myself and value the work done.
